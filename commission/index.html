How Commission Works

Baccarat pays a 5% commission on all winning Banker bets.

Step 1 — Commission: multiply the Banker bet by 5% (× 0.05).
Step 2 — Net payout: subtract the commission from the original bet.

Example: $200 bet → commission $10.00 → net payout $190.00.

Round to the nearest cent. Use the Enter key or the Check button to submit each step.
